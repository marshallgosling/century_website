GPU Cloud Pricing FAQ

How does Century Litong reduce costs?

At Century Litong we focus on delivering the best performance-for-cost. We offer highly competitive on-demand pricing, a broad selection of NVIDIA GPUs so you can choose the best fit for your workload, fast and flexible infrastructure, and excellent support to help optimize your stack for both performance and cost.

Do you offer discounts?

Many customers benefit from our on-demand pricing. For workloads with predictable usage patterns we offer discounted plans — contact our sales team to discuss options tailored to your business.

Are bandwidth or data transfer charges applied?

In most use cases we do not charge for regional transfers, workstation data, or egress. If you have very large bandwidth needs we will work with you to understand requirements and negotiate consolidated bandwidth rates with our suppliers.
